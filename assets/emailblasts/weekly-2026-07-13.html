Soap and sanitizer
The CDC notes that proper handwashing with soap and water removes germs more effectively than sanitizer alone when hands are visibly dirty, which is why stocked wash stations still matter even in a sanitizer-heavy setup.
Keep refills close so employees and customers never hit an empty pump at the wrong time.

Trash can liners
A trash liner is strongest when the bag size matches the can closely. Too much overhang increases drag on the rim and makes punctures or split seams more likely during removal.
The right liner size and strength helps crews swap faster and avoid leaks when pickup volume jumps.
