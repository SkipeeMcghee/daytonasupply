Office
Window envelopes save a step because the address on the document shows through the front, which is one reason they are still everywhere in billing, statements, and routine office mail.

Soap and sanitizer
Soap usually works better with a little time than extra force. A few extra seconds of lathering helps loosen oils and grime much better than a fast pump-and-rinse.
A steady supply of soap and sanitizer helps restrooms, break rooms, and customer-facing areas stay ready without turning a five-minute refill into a very visible problem.
"I work in conflict resolution. Dirt gets attached, and I help everyone move on."

Poly bags
Most poly bags are made from branched polyethylene, which stays flexible at thin gauges while resisting moisture, making it useful when you need visibility and protection without adding much weight.
We stock poly bag options that help teams sort parts, protect merchandise, and pack smaller items faster when a full carton would be more material than the job actually needs.
