Stretch film
Stretch film holds a load together because the plastic wants to return to its original shape. That pullback force keeps boxes tighter on the pallet even after vibration, turns, and trailer movement.

Corrugated boxes
Corrugated board gets much of its strength from the fluted middle layer. Those arches help a lightweight box resist stacking pressure better than a flat sheet of the same paper would.
